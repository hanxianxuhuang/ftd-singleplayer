Objects

  1. Purple Square: Obstacle
  2. Yellow Square: Box
  3. Blue Square: Player
  4. Red Square: Enemy
  5. Green Text: Health

Operations

  1. Restart with t, Pause with p, Resumn with either r or wasd
  2. Move with wasd
  3. Boxes restore bullets and health, Move next to them and press e
  4. Aim with your mouse
  5. Mouse click fires

Features

  1. No friendly fire
  2. The speed of bullets is affected by the location of the mouse (further-->faster && closer-->slower)
  3. The damage of bullets is affected by how many obstacles are around (1 obstacle-->×2 && 2 obstacles-->×3 && 3 obstacles-->×4)
